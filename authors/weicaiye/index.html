Biography

I’m a fourth-year(2018-) Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Guofeng Zhang. I'm also affiliated with 3D Vision Group, State Key Lab of CAD & CG, Zhejiang University (ZJU). I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) with honor in 2018.

My research area lies in the field of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Machine Learning, particularly in 3D Vision. My research goal is to make computers (robotics) learn to perceive, localize, reconstruct, reason, and interact with the real world.
