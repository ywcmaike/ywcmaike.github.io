Biography

I’m a fifth-year (2018-) Ph.D. student in the 3D Vision Group, State Key Lab of CAD & CG, Zhejiang University (ZJU), supervised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Guofeng Zhang. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2018.

Currently, I am a visiting researcher in the Computer Vision and Geometry Group (CVG), ETH Zurich, advised by Prof. Marc Pollefeys. My research area lies in the field of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Machine Learning, particularly in 3D Vision. My research goal is to make computers (robotics) learn to perceive, localize, reconstruct, reason, and interact with the real world.

Experience

3D Reconstruction of Indoor Scene of RGB-D Images
Developed SemanticSLAM system which organically combines the traditional SLAM, semantic segmentation, and plane detection. Different modules cooperate with each other and effectively improve the quality of localization and mapping. The system allows room-scale indoor scenes reconstruction in real-time.
3D Vision Researcher Intern Jan,2018--May,2018 Advisor: Dr. Hanqing Jiang
Video Search System
Developed millisecond response video search services with colleagues that can support hundreds of millions of highly concurrent retrieval needs. Developed rearrangement strategies, such as video resolution and cross-modal fusion with colleagues, to improve the quality of video retrieval results. The system can retrieve videos related to a given text in real time and the service is available.
Software Engineer Intern Feb,2017--Jul,2017
